The perfect trip

I would see scenes like the bustling streets of Shibuya Crossing, the serene temples in Kyoto, and the breathtaking view of Mount Fuji from Lake Kawaguchi. I’d also explore the beautiful cherry blossom gardens during springtime, and the vibrant colors of autumn in the Japanese countryside.

And animals like the playful snow monkeys in Jigokudani, the majestic cranes in Hokkaido, and the friendly deer roaming freely in Nara Park. I might even catch a glimpse of some sea creatures while visiting the famous Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium.

And eat food like sushi from Tsukiji Fish Market, ramen from a cozy shop in Fukuoka, and street food like takoyaki and okonomiyaki in Osaka. I’d also enjoy traditional kaiseki dinners and try unique dishes like wagyu beef and matcha-flavored desserts.
